## Mileage — The Core Question

Both bikes are purchased primarily for commuting efficiency. Let's be precise.

**Hero Splendor Plus**: Widely reported to achieve 65–70 kmpl in Telangana's real-world conditions. Some Kamareddy users report touching 68–70 kmpl on smooth sections of the Kamareddy-Nizamabad highway.

**Honda Shine 100**: Consistently achieves 60–63 kmpl in similar conditions. The fuel-injected version (available in most 2026 variants) brings slightly better cold-start efficiency than carbureted alternatives.

**The difference**: ~4–5 kmpl in favor of Splendor Plus.

At ₹112/litre petrol and 1,200 km/month:
- Splendor Plus monthly fuel: ~₹2,020 (at 66 kmpl)
- Shine 100 monthly fuel: ~₹2,240 (at 60 kmpl)
- Monthly saving with Splendor: ~₹220

Over 3 years: Hero Splendor saves ~₹7,920 in fuel vs Shine 100. That's a meaningful figure, though not overwhelming.

## Build Quality & Refinement

This is where Honda distinguishes itself clearly.

**Honda Shine 100**: The 2026 Shine 100 feels tighter, better assembled, and more refined than the Splendor Plus. Panel gaps are tighter, the engine vibrates less at idle, and the overall quality feel is a notch above.

Honda's Japanese quality standards are visible in:
- Smoother gear shifts (6 gears vs 4 gears on Splendor)
- Quieter engine at high rpm
- Better paint quality and finish
- More consistent build across units

**Hero Splendor Plus**: Proven, durable, but the engine can feel slightly rough compared to Shine 100, especially at higher rpm. The 4-speed gearbox is simpler and more familiar to riders in rural Kamareddy — fewer gears to manage.

## Engine & Riding Feel

### Honda Shine 100's Advantage: 6 Gears

The Shine 100 has 6 gears — unusual for a 100cc class bike. This means:
- Lower rpm at highway speeds (less strain on engine)
- Better fuel efficiency at sustained speeds
- More gear options for varying road conditions

On the Kamareddy-Banswada road with its mixed terrain, Shine 100 riders can select the right gear more precisely.

### Hero Splendor Plus's Advantage: Simplicity

Splendor's 4-speed gearbox is simple and thoroughly proven. In remote areas near Tadwai, Pitlam, or Bibipet where local mechanics are more familiar with Hero than Honda, Splendor's simple mechanicals are easier to fix with basic tools.
