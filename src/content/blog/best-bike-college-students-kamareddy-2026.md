## What a Student Needs from a Bike

Before recommending specific models, here's what matters most for Kamareddy college students:

1. **Low fuel cost**: Petrol is your biggest monthly expense after college fees
2. **Easy maintenance**: Simple bikes cost less to service
3. **Light weight**: Easier to handle in traffic and for parking
4. **Affordable EMI**: Parents can afford the monthly payment
5. **Looks matter**: Let's be honest — style counts in college
6. **Reliability**: Missing college because your bike broke down is not an option
