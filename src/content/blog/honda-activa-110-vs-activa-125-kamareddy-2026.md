## Honda Activa 110 vs Activa 125 — Which to Buy in Kamareddy?

"Activa కొనాలి — కానీ 110 తీసుకోవాలా, 125 తీసుకోవాలా?" This is easily the most common question at Prakash Auto Honda in Kamareddy. Every week, families come in having already decided on Activa — but uncertain which variant makes more sense.

The price gap is real: roughly ₹15,000 separates these two scooters. Is the 125 worth the extra money for Kamareddy buyers? Let's answer that properly.

## The ₹15,000 Question

The Activa 125 costs roughly ₹15,000 more. Where does that money go?

1. **Bigger engine** (124cc vs 109.51cc) — more power, better highway performance
2. **12-inch wheels** (vs 10-inch) — better stability at speed, handles potholes better
3. **Higher ground clearance** (171mm vs 155mm) — less scraping on speed bumps
4. **OBD2-compliant PGM-FI** engine (fuel injection standard on 125)
5. **Alloy wheels** standard (some 110 variants have alloy too — check at showroom)

---

## Engine & Performance: Where the Gap Shows

### Activa 110 in Kamareddy

The 110's 109.51cc engine is beautifully matched to Kamareddy town use. At 30–50 kmph town speeds, it feels eager and responsive. Petrol is used very efficiently — real-world **54–57 kmpl** from Kamareddy customers.

For purely in-town commuting (home → market → school → back), the 110 does everything you need and does it efficiently.

### Activa 125 on Kamareddy Roads

The 124cc engine makes a notable difference in two scenarios that Kamareddy riders encounter regularly:

**Scenario 1 — Kamareddy-Nizamabad highway (65km)**: The 125's extra torque (10.5 Nm vs 9.09 Nm) means it settles into highway cruising at 65–70 kmph without straining. The 110 can reach those speeds but feels more worked at sustained highway speeds.

**Scenario 2 — Two-up riding**: Kamareddy families often ride scooters with two adults (or adult + child). With a pillion, the 125's extra power and torque makes a meaningful difference — overtaking is easier, climbs on the Banswada road feel less laborious.

**Scenario 3 — Speed bumps and rough patches**: The 125's 171mm ground clearance (vs 155mm on 110) means far fewer bottom-outs on Kamareddy's notorious speed bumps and rough railway crossings.

## 12-inch vs 10-inch Wheels — A Big Deal in Kamareddy

This is an underrated difference. The Activa 125's 12-inch wheels vs Activa 110's 10-inch wheels matters significantly on Kamareddy's roads:

- **Better pothole absorption**: Larger wheels roll over surface irregularities more smoothly
- **Better straight-line stability**: At 60+ kmph, 12-inch wheels track straighter
- **Better in monsoon**: Larger contact patch gives more grip on wet roads — Kamareddy's monsoon roads can be treacherous

Families who use the scooter for school runs (kids pillion) or weekend Banswada trips frequently report the 125 feels more "planted" and reassuring than the 110.

## Features — What the 125 Gets Extra

The Activa 125 in 2026 comes with:
- Standard fuel injection (PGM-FI) — smoother, cleaner, better throttle response
- Honda's ACG (AC Generator) starter for silent, smooth starts
- External fuel filling (fill petrol without opening the seat)
- Fully alloy wheels standard

The Activa 110 in 2026:
- May come with fuel injection on select variants (check at showroom — OBD2 models have FI)
- ACG silent starter on all variants
- External fuel filling — yes, same feature
- Alloy wheels on most variants

## Who Should Buy Activa 110

- **Budget is fixed at ₹77,000–₹85,000 on-road**
- **Purely urban use** — short distances within Kamareddy town
- **Senior riders** who prefer the lighter, slightly smaller form factor
- **Second vehicle** in a family (first scooter is already a 125)
- Riders who cover fewer than 600km/month

## Who Should Buy Activa 125

- **Regular highway riders** — especially Kamareddy-Nizamabad (65km) or Kamareddy-Banswada
- **Two-up riders** — families who regularly carry pillion
- **Young professionals** who want the premium feel
- Riders covering 1,000+ km/month (better highway capability offsets slightly higher fuel cost)
- Those who'll keep the scooter 4+ years (better resale value)

---

## Resale Value in Kamareddy's Second-Hand Market

Activa 125 consistently commands ₹8,000–₹12,000 more than Activa 110 of the same age in Kamareddy's used vehicle market. A 3-year-old Activa 125 typically sells for ₹65,000–₹72,000, while an Activa 110 of the same age fetches ₹55,000–₹62,000.

Factor this in: the effective extra cost of the 125 is closer to ₹5,000–₹7,000 over 3 years — not ₹15,000.

## The Verdict

**Buy Activa 110 if**: You're a pure town commuter in Kamareddy with a tight budget and no highway needs. The 55+ kmpl mileage and lower price make it a smart, practical choice.

**Buy Activa 125 if**: You travel regularly on Kamareddy-Nizamabad highway, ride two-up frequently, or want the best Activa experience for the next 4–5 years. The ₹15,000 premium is genuinely justified.

For most Kamareddy families — especially those with any highway riding needs — we recommend the **Activa 125**. The 12-inch wheels, higher ground clearance, and better highway performance make it the safer, more capable choice.
