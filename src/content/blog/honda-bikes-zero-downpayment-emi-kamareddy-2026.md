## What Is Zero Down Payment?

A **zero down payment** loan means you don't pay anything upfront at the showroom (except for registration, insurance, and accessories — those are paid separately or included in the loan based on the lender).

**Regular loan**: You pay 10–20% of the ex-showroom price upfront, finance the rest.

**Zero down payment**: You finance 100% of the ex-showroom price. Monthly EMI is slightly higher, but you don't need to arrange any lump-sum amount.

> Note: Zero down payment applies to the ex-showroom price. On-road costs like registration (~₹3,000–₹5,000) and insurance (~₹8,000–₹12,000) may need to be paid upfront or added to the loan depending on the lender.
