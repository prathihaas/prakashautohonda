## Telangana Road Tax — How It Works

In Telangana, road tax for two-wheelers is based on engine capacity and ex-showroom price:

- **Up to 50cc**: 9% of cost
- **51cc–125cc**: 9% of cost (Activa 110 and 125 fall here)
- **Above 125cc**: 9% of cost

Road tax is a one-time payment paid at the time of registration. Unlike annual tax in some other states, Telangana's road tax payment is made once and is permanent.

## Insurance Cost Breakdown

Insurance for a new Honda Activa includes:

- **Third-party insurance** (mandatory by law): ~₹538/year for 110cc, ~₹714/year for 125cc (IRDAI regulated rates)
- **Own damage insurance** (comprehensive cover): Varies by IDV, chosen company, and add-ons

First-year comprehensive insurance typically bundled with purchase: ₹9,000–₹12,500 depending on variant and insurer.

**Renewal insurance** from 2nd year onwards:
- Third-party only: ₹538–₹714/year
- Comprehensive: ₹3,000–₹5,000/year depending on IDV and NCB (No Claim Bonus)
