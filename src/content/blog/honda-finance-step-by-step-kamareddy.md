## Step 2: Decide Loan Structure

**How much down payment can you manage?**

- **Zero down payment**: Finance 100% of ex-showroom price (subject to eligibility; higher EMI)
- **10% down**: Standard comfortable starting point
- **20% down**: Better — lower EMI, lower interest burden
- **30%+ down**: Least interest overall

**Choose your tenure (loan period):**

| Tenure | EMI | Total Interest |
|--------|-----|----------------|
| 24 months | Higher EMI | Less interest |
| 36 months | Moderate | Moderate |
| 48 months | Lower EMI | Most interest |

**General rule**: Choose the shortest tenure where EMI doesn't exceed 20–25% of your monthly income.

## Step 5: Lender Verification & Approval

After submission, the lender does their verification:

### What They Verify
- **CIBIL credit score**: Score 650+ makes approval smoother; lower scores may need guarantor
- **Income verification**: Bank statement is cross-checked against declared income
- **Document authenticity**: Aadhaar/PAN details verified digitally
- **Physical verification**: Some lenders may call your employer or do a field visit (for larger loans)

### Timeline
- **Same day approval**: Common for straightforward cases with good credit score and complete documents
- **24 hours**: Standard for most Kamareddy applications
- **48 hours**: Complex cases or if additional documents needed
- **Beyond 48 hours**: Rare; usually means an issue needs resolution

### Improving Approval Speed
- Submit complete documents the first time (avoid incomplete applications)
- Include 6-month bank statement (more is better than minimum 3-month)
- If your CIBIL score is below 650, bring a co-applicant from the start (don't wait for rejection)

## Step 6: Sign Loan Agreement

Once approved, you'll receive a **loan sanction letter** from the lender specifying:
- Approved loan amount
- Interest rate
- EMI amount
- Tenure
- Processing fees

**Read this carefully before signing.** Check:
- EMI matches what you were quoted
- Interest rate matches quoted rate
- No unexpected fees added

Our team at Prakash Auto Honda explains every line item — don't hesitate to ask questions.

Sign the loan agreement and related forms. A physical signature or biometric Aadhaar eSign may be required depending on the lender.

## Step 7: Down Payment and Insurance

At the time of delivery, you'll need to arrange:

1. **Down payment**: Your agreed upfront payment (₹0 for zero-down-payment loans, or your chosen amount)
2. **Insurance premium**: First-year comprehensive insurance (₹9,000–₹12,500 depending on model — can often be added to loan)
3. **Registration costs**: Road tax + RTO registration (₹8,000–₹12,000 depending on model — sometimes financed)

**Total cash needed at delivery** (example for Activa 125 with 10% down payment):
- Down payment: ₹9,200
- Insurance: ₹11,000 (if not in loan)
- Registration: ~₹9,700 (if not in loan)
- **Total approx**: ₹29,900

Ask our team to include insurance and registration in the loan amount — many buyers prefer this to minimize day-of-delivery cash requirements.

## After Loan — Important Reminders

- **First EMI**: Usually due 30 days after disbursement — check exact date in sanction letter
- **ECS mandate**: Most lenders use ECS auto-debit from your bank account — ensure sufficient balance monthly
- **Prepayment**: You can close the loan early (usually after 6 months without penalty; check terms)
- **RC book with hypothecation**: Your bike's RC will show lender's name — this is normal and doesn't affect usage
- **NOC after full repayment**: Get the NOC from lender and submit to RTO for updated RC
