## Mileage & Performance — Telangana Daily Use Reality

All three manufacturers claim 60–65 kmpl, but Telangana riders experience different numbers.

**Honda Activa 6G:** Honda's PGM-FI on a 110cc platform delivers **52–57 kmpl** consistently. The lighter weight (107 kg), Honda Eco Technology, and well-calibrated fuel injection make it the most predictably efficient.

**Suzuki Access 125:** Suzuki claims 65 kmpl but real-world reports from Telangana-based riders show **50–56 kmpl**. It has 15cc more displacement than the Activa 6G, which means slightly more power but also slightly more fuel consumption. For highway rides to Nizamabad, the Access 125 feels more responsive.

**TVS Jupiter:** At 110cc with TVS's CVTi technology, real-world mileage is **50–55 kmpl** in Kamareddy conditions. TVS Jupiter is slightly lighter than the Access but has the smallest fuel tank (5L) — more frequent fuel stops on longer rides.

**For Kamareddy daily commuters (15–25 km/day):**
Fuel cost difference between the three is minimal — ₹1,000–₹2,500/year. The bigger financial decisions are service cost and resale value (see below).

**Riding comfort:** Suzuki Access 125 wins on highway comfort thanks to 125cc engine and slightly more legroom. TVS Jupiter wins on boot space (22L). Honda Activa 6G wins on overall build quality feel and ride confidence.
