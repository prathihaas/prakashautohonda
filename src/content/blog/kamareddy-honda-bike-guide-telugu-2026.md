## On-Road Price అంటే ఏమిటి?

Showroom లో quote చేసే price అనేది ex-showroom price. మీరు actually pay చేసే total amount దానికంటే ఎక్కువ ఉంటుంది. దాని breakdown:

| Component | ఎంత |
|-----------|------|
| Ex-showroom price | (bike price) |
| Road Tax (Telangana, ~9%) | Ex-showroom × 9% |
| Registration | ₹1,200–₹1,500 |
| Insurance | ₹9,500–₹12,500 |
| Smart Card | ₹400 |
| **Total On-Road** | **₹10,000–₹17,000 more** |

**Example: Honda SP 125**
- Ex-showroom: ₹95,000
- Road Tax: ~₹8,550
- Registration: ₹1,500
- Insurance: ₹11,500
- **Total On-Road: ~₹1,07,000**
