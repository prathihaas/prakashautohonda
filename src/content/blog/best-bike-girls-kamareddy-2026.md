## Safety Features to Look for

All recommended Honda bikes above include:
- **CBS (Combined Braking System)**: Applying rear brake automatically applies some front brake too — significantly reduces braking distance and prevents wheel lock-up in panic stops
- **LED headlamp**: Better night visibility — important for evening commutes
- **Tubeless tyres**: A flat won't immediately cause a fall — you can ride slowly to a shop

## Riding Tips for New Women Riders in Kamareddy

1. **Start in a quiet area**: Kamareddy's stadium parking or early morning on the bypass road — get comfortable with the bike before hitting traffic
2. **Practice parking**: Backward parking on a slope is the hardest — practice until it's natural
3. **Dress appropriately**: Closed shoes, no flowing fabric near wheels
4. **Night riding**: Use high beam on rural roads, stay visible to other vehicles
5. **Service on schedule**: Honda's 6,000 km service interval should be kept — it ensures reliability
