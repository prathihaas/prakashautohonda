## Service Center Visit Tips

### 1. Book in Advance
Walk-in service is accepted, but booking in advance via WhatsApp ensures:
- Shorter waiting time
- Specific technician availability for complex repairs
- Parts pre-ordered if needed (avoids a second visit)

**Book via WhatsApp**: **[WhatsApp us](https://wa.me/919030857333)**

### 2. Know Your Service Interval
Honda's recommended service interval is **6,000 km or 6 months** — whichever comes first. Don't wait for a problem to appear. Regular preventive service keeps your bike reliable.

### 3. Bring Your Service Book
Every Honda comes with a service book. Bring it to every service visit. It:
- Records all services performed
- Is required for warranty claims
- Increases resale value (buyers in Kamareddy's second-hand market pay more for service-history bikes)

### 4. Use Genuine Honda Engine Oil
Honda Genuine 4-stroke engine oil is specifically formulated for Honda's engines. Using it at every service helps maintain engine performance and prolongs life. Ask specifically for Honda Genuine oil at service.

### 5. First Service at 500 km — Don't Skip It
The first free service at 500–1,000 km is critical. During the running-in period, metal particles from new engine components settle. The oil change at first service removes these — skipping it can cause early engine wear.

### 6. Annual Checks Beyond Routine Service
Every year (or if you cover 15,000+ km/year), ask for:
- Brake fluid replacement
- Battery terminal inspection and cleaning
- All fasteners torque check
- Tyre tread depth measurement
