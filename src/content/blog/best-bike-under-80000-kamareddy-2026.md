## #1 — Honda Shine 100 (~₹76,000)

**Best for**: Mileage, long-distance daily commuting, value buyers

The [Honda Shine 100](/products/shine-100) is our top pick in this segment for most Kamareddy riders. Here's why:

**Mileage**: 60–63 kmpl — the highest mileage in any Honda motorcycle. At ₹112/litre petrol and 1,200 km/month, monthly fuel cost is approximately ₹2,165. That's less than ₹75/day for your daily commuting.

**6-Speed gearbox**: Unusual for a 100cc class bike. The 6 gears mean the engine runs at lower, more relaxed revs on the Kamareddy-Nizamabad highway — quieter, smoother, marginally more efficient.

**Weight**: 107kg — the lightest Honda motorcycle. Easy to handle in Kamareddy town traffic and simple to park.

**Build quality**: Honda quality at an entry price point. Tight panel fits, reliable paint, durable components.

**Monthly EMI (36 months, 20% down)**: ~₹2,000

**Perfect for**: Daily commuters from villages to Kamareddy town, students, farmers covering regular distances, anyone prioritizing fuel savings above all else.

## #2 — Honda Activa 110 (~₹77,000)

**Best for**: Family scooter, women riders, effortless daily use

The [Honda Activa 110](/products/activa-110) is India's best-selling scooter — and the top choice for families in Kamareddy who want a reliable, versatile vehicle.

**Automatic gearbox**: Zero clutch work. Pure twist-and-go. Ideal for mixed riders in a family (mom, dad, student all use it). No learning curve.

**Storage**: 18.2 litres under-seat storage — fits a full-face helmet, tiffin box, and small bag. More practical than any 100cc motorcycle.

**Mileage**: 54–57 kmpl — slightly lower than Shine 100, but still excellent for daily use.

**Safety**: CBS (Combined Braking System) standard — both wheels braked simultaneously in emergencies. Particularly valuable in Kamareddy's unpredictable traffic.

**Monthly EMI (36 months, 20% down)**: ~₹2,020

**Perfect for**: Families needing one reliable vehicle for multiple riders; housewives, senior riders; school-run vehicles; anyone who prefers automatic convenience.

## #3 — Honda Dio 110 (~₹73,000)

**Best for**: Young riders who want sporty styling at entry price

The Honda Dio 110 brings sporty design at the most affordable price in Honda's scooter lineup. At ~₹73,000, it's ₹4,000 cheaper than Activa 110 with a more youthful character.

**Design**: The Dio 110's angular, forward-leaning design appeals strongly to young riders. Available in vibrant colors with sporty graphics.

**Engine**: 109.51cc (same as Activa 110) — proven reliable, 52–55 kmpl mileage.

**Weight**: Lightest Honda scooter at ~105kg — very easy to handle.

**What's the compromise?**: Smaller storage capacity vs Activa 110. Lower seat height (better for shorter riders).

**Monthly EMI (36 months, 20% down)**: ~₹1,920

**Perfect for**: College students who want a stylish scooter; first-time riders; shorter riders who prefer a lower seat height; anyone prioritizing style at the lowest price.

## #4 — Honda Livo (~₹80,000)

**Best for**: Style + comfort in a motorcycle under ₹80,000

The [Honda Livo](/products/livo) is the most stylish motorcycle in this price range. At ~₹80,000 (just at our budget ceiling), it offers a more premium look and feel than Shine 100.

**Design**: Chrome accents, body-colored mirrors, stylish dual-tone color options. More head-turning than Shine 100 in Kamareddy's streets.

**Engine**: 109.51cc — slightly larger than Shine 100's 99.7cc. More torque (9.09 Nm) means better two-up performance.

**Mileage**: ~60 kmpl — slightly lower than Shine 100's 62 kmpl but negligibly different in daily use.

**ACG Silent Starter**: Honda's Alternating Current Generator starter = near-silent engine start. No kickstarter clunk. Impresses every morning.

**Monthly EMI (36 months, 20% down)**: ~₹2,100

**Perfect for**: Riders aged 25–40 who want a stylish, comfortable daily motorcycle; anyone who uses the bike for image-conscious daily commuting; pillion-heavy riders who need the extra torque.
